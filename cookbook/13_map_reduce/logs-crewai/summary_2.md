This report concludes that the temporary equilibrium of centralized AI inference is shifting due to easing hardware scarcity, advancements in model optimization, and the imminent solution to the critical coordination problem. It advocates for an open standard protocol – analogous to SMTP and HTTP – to foster interoperability and prevent vendor lock-in, suggesting independent projects and startups are best positioned to drive its development. The report recommends a provider-agnostic architectural approach for enterprises, emphasizing flexibility and hybrid execution strategies based on cost, availability, and workload requirements, citing key statistical references to support its arguments.
