    INPUT: @topic TEXT
    OUTPUT: @summary TEXT

    -- Step 1: Research
    GENERATE research(@topic) INTO @research

    -- Step 2: Analyze

    -- Step 3: Summarize
    GENERATE summarize(@analysis) INTO @summary

    COMMIT @summary WITH status = 'complete'
END

**Types of Distributed AI Inference:**

1. **Distributed Training**: Multiple machines work together to train a single AI model on large-scale datasets.

3. **Edge Computing**: AI models are run on edge devices (e.g., smartphones, smart home devices) to reduce latency and improve security.
