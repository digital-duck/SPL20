My opponent's assertion that open-sourcing AI would guarantee transparency, accountability, and verifiability rings hollow when considering the significant security risks associated with making cutting-edge technology freely available to the public. This would stifle innovation and progress, rather than promoting it. By preserving the secrecy surrounding AI development, we can protect the integrity of this critical technology and ensure that its benefits are shared equitably among all stakeholders.
